### Nice to meet you!

Hello, I'm Zhenlin Zhu, a Chinese girl born in Sichuan.
Thanks to my dad who bought the first telescope at home, I was obsessed with the stunning night sky since childhood. Following my curiosity in the stars and universe, I did my bachelor and master in Department of Astronomy and Space Science at Nanjing University. After that, I went to Netherlands and continued my journey at Leiden University to get my PhD. I worked at Netherlands Institute for Space Research (SRON) for 5 years, focusing on the X-ray emission of galaxy clusters.
